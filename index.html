    About Me

    I'm a PhD student at the University of Texas at San Antonio. I am part of Advance Data Engineering Lab and advised by Professor Dr. Adel Alaeddini.

    My research interests lie at Graphical Models specially Bayesian Networks, scalable graph mining and time series mining. Currently, I am working on Multi-Level Bayesian Network to detect patterns of Multiple Chronic Conditions in patients.
